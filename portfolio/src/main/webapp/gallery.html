My Gallery

Photography is the way I capture moments of the world around me. Photos allow me to remmeber important moments and people by providing me with a reminder of them. Most of my photos fall into one of three categories.

Important Moments

These photos were originally taken in the moment without any thought, but looking back on them they are what will keep me from forgetting experiences that changed my life.

My Cat

Gracie is the family cat. She's seven years old and throughout those years she has never failed to be a great model for photographs. These are only a few of the many photos I have of her.

Drawing Inspiration

These are photos that may not look like much, but when I took then I was trying to capture an element of the environment that I wanted to incorporate into a drawing. Recently I have had more time to practice drawing and so these photos serve have been serving as inspiration for what I draw.
